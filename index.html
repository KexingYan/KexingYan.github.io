ECO225 · Final Project

Kiva Microfinance & Economic Development

Constructed a panel dataset spanning 84 countries (2013–2017), 357 observations by merging Kiva, World Bank, and institutional datasets. Analyzed the relationship between GDP per capita and microfinance activity, built an institutional quality index via PCA, and conducted OLS regressions with interaction terms on millions of loan-level observations.
